Some common questions
were often asked

How do I book an appointment?

You can easily book an appointment with a mental health professional by signing up, browsing therapists, and selecting a date and time.

How can I find a specialized therapist of my problem?

Our system recommends to you with a search for therapists based on their specialization and expertise using KNN algorithm.

Do I need a doctor referral to book?

No, you do not need a referral from your doctor to book an appointment with a mental health professional through our system.

How soon can I book an appointment?

The availability of appointments varies depending on the therapist's schedule, but you can generally find available appointments within a few days or weeks.

How do I know if my appointment is confirmed?

Your status in dashboard will change once your appointment has been booked and confirmed.

Can I book regular appointments with the same therapist?

Yes, you can choose to book appointments with the same therapist on a recurring basis or as needed.
